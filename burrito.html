Cheesy Potato Burrito

The Taco Bell Cheesy Potato Burrito is filled with seasoned beef, cheese, sour cream and seasoned potato bites.

Ingredients

  • 4 flour tortillas
  • 75g cheese dip
  • 80ml sour cream
  • 140g minced beef
  1. Reheat potatoes at 204 C for 8-10 minutes, if needed (otherwise use hot potatoes).
  2. Fry 140g of minced beef according to taco seasoning packet instructions.
  3. Microwave cheese dip for 30-45 seconds and stir to smooth out.
  4. Assemble tortillas; dollop 2 teaspoons of cheese and 2 teaspoons of sour cream onto the lower half of each tortilla. Top with potato bites and spoon of ground beef.
  5. Roll and microwave for 30 seconds. Serve hot.
