Mushroom & Bacon

A mushroom side dish that is full of flavour.

Ingredients

  • 4 strips of bacon
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 660g mushrooms
  • 60ml water
  • 15g parsley
  1. Heat a saucepan on thr stove and add the chopped bacon. Cook for 5-7 minutes until its almost cooked through.
  2. Add garlic and cook until it is no longer raw and the bacon has finished cooking.
  3. Add mushrooms and stir well to coat them with bacon goodness.
  4. Pour in water and use to de-glaze the pan.
  5. Cook the mushrooms uncovered, stirring occasionally until they have cooked through.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley.
