Mushroom & Bacon

A mushroom side dish that is full of flavour.

Ingredients

4 strips of bacon

6 cloves of garlic

660g mushrooms

60ml water

15g parsley

  • Heat a saucepan on thr stove and add the chopped bacon. Cook for 5-7 minutes until its almost cooked through.
  • Add garlic and cook until it is no longer raw and the bacon has finished cooking.
  • Add mushrooms and stir well to coat them with bacon goodness.
  • Pour in water and use to de-glaze the pan.
  • Cook the mushrooms uncovered, stirring occasionally until they have cooked through.
  • Garnish with chopped parsley.
