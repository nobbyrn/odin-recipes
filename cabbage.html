Bacon wrapped cabbage

A mushroom side dish that is full of flavour.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cabbage
  • 450g bacon
  • Black pepper
  1. Preheat over 190 C
  2. Slice cabbage into wedges or large chunks.
  3. Wrap each cabbage with bacon, secure with toothpick.
  4. Place into a roasting pan. Bake for 30 minutes. Flip cabbage over and bake for a further 15 minutes or until cabbage is tender and bacon is crisp.
